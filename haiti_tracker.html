HaitiLibre
[...] no fuel shortage has been observed on the national market. Petroleum products are available at gas pumps and their distribution is ensured through formally authorized channels. Consequently, any sale of fuel outside these channels, particularly in jerrycans or any other container not compliant with the law, is strictly prohibited throughout the national territory. As such, any individual or legal entity involved in the illegal sale of fuel will be subject to immediate enforcement measures, including the seizure of products, the closure of the sales outlets concerned, and legal proceedings in accordance with applicable laws.' (James Monazard, Minister of Commerce and Industry)
